About Us

At HealthTech Digital Solutions, we are committed to revolutionizing healthcare through cutting-edge digital transformation. Our mission is to empower hospitals, clinics, and medical institutions by integrating innovative technologies that enhance patient care, streamline workflows, and optimize operational efficiency.

With a deep understanding of both healthcare and digital advancements, our team provides tailored solutions that address the unique challenges faced by medical organizations. From electronic health records (EHR) integration to AI-powered diagnostics and process automation, we help institutions stay at the forefront of modern healthcare.

We believe that technology should serve people—not the other way around. That’s why we work closely with healthcare providers to implement seamless, user-friendly digital solutions that improve both patient outcomes and administrative efficiency.

Our CEO

Dr. Abelardo Bermeo is an expert professional in healthcare technology, specializing in digital transformation and quality management in health services. With a background in medical practice and a specialization in healthcare management and auditing, he has successfully led initiatives that integrate technology into clinical and administrative processes to enhance efficiency and patient care.

As a visionary in health informatics, he has worked on projects ranging from data-driven decision-making tools to the development of digital platforms for optimizing service delivery. His expertise includes implementing technological solutions that improve healthcare accessibility, compliance with regulatory standards, and overall institutional performance.
