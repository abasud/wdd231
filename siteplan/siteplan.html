Project Site Plan

Site Name: HealthTech Digital Solutions

This name represents a company dedicated to driving digital transformation in healthcare institutions. "HealthTech" highlights the integration of technology in healthcare, while "Digital Solutions" emphasizes the innovative services provided.

Site Purpose:

HealthTech Digital Solutions provides specialized digital transformation services for healthcare institutions, including hospitals, clinics, and medical centers. The site showcases the importance of implementing advanced technologies in patient care, workflow automation, and operational efficiency. It will offer insights into digital healthcare trends, service offerings, and client testimonials.

Scenarios:

  • How can digital transformation improve patient care in hospitals?
  • What are the best technologies for automating administrative processes in healthcare institutions?

Color Schema:

  • Primary Color: #592941, used for headings, navigation bar, and key accents to convey professionalism and trust.
  • Secondary Color: #498467, used for background highlights, buttons, and call-to-action elements to create a modern and clean look.
  • First Accent Color: #52B788, used for icons, links, and minor details to symbolize health and innovation.
  • Second Accent Color: #B2D3A8, used for highlights and emphasis divs to maintain readability and aesthetic balance.
  • Third Accent Color: #EDE5A6, used for subtle background elements to provide contrast and enhance user experience.

Typography:

  • Heading Font: "Montserrat", a modern and bold sans-serif font, used for titles and headings.
  • Body Font: "Lato", clean and readable sans-serif font, used for paragraphs and descriptions.
  • Accent Font: "Roboto", used for buttons, call-to-action text, and emphasis divs.
